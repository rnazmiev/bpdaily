# BP Daily — Data Deletion Request

**App:** BP Daily
**Developer:** Rushan Nazmiev

## What data is stored

BP Daily stores your blood pressure readings, reminders, and settings **only on your device** (local SQLite database). This data never leaves your phone and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app or clear app storage in Android Settings.

## What data is collected by third parties

We use **Adapty** to manage subscriptions. Adapty stores:
- An anonymous installation ID
- Device model and OS version
- Subscription purchase events

No personal data (name, email, health data) is collected.

## How to request deletion

To delete your Adapty profile data, email:

**nazmievjob@gmail.com**

Subject: `Delete my data`

Include your installation ID (found in **Settings → About** inside the app).

We process deletion requests within 30 days.

## How to delete local data immediately

1. Uninstall BP Daily, **or**
2. Android Settings → Apps → BP Daily → Storage → Clear data.

This permanently removes all readings, reminders, and settings from your device.
